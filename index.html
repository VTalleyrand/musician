Jameson Morter is a well seasoned guitarist with an international track record. Growing up in a jazz-obsessed household, Jameson picked up his first guitar at the age of 6. After founding his first indie-rock band with a few of his high school mates and gaining traction during the MySpace era, the group decided to dissolve after a successful European tour to focus on solo careers. For the past 5 years Jameson has been composing melodies for the local tv network and giving private lessons to kids.

UPDATE I


Dear fans, thank you for supporting me and my music during these hard times. To raise funds for the victims of COVID-19, me and my old group members from Pine Trees In The Desert decided to come together one more time for a digital live performance from the comfort of our homes. The live will be streamed on our YouTube page and all proceeds will be donated to the local hospital of Durham North Carolina.
